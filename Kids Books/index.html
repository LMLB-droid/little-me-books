Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Little Me Books.

How does the AI personalisation actually work? +
When you upload a photo, our AI analyses your child's facial features, skin tone, hair colour and style. It then adapts every illustration in the book so the main character is a storybook-style representation of your child. The result looks naturally hand-illustrated — not a photo paste-in.
Is my child's photo kept private? +
Absolutely. Your child's photo is used solely to generate the personalised illustrations. Once the book files are created, the original photo is permanently deleted from our servers. We never store, share, or use photos for any other purpose.
How long does delivery take? +
Most orders are processed and printed within 2–3 business days, then shipped via our Book Vault partnership. Standard delivery typically takes 5–7 business days. Express options are available at checkout for many countries.
What if I'm not happy with how it looks? +
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the personalisation, contact us before approving your order and we'll re-generate the illustrations for free. If you receive a damaged or defective book, we'll reprint and reship at no cost.
What age range are the books for? +
Our books are designed for children aged 2–8. Each title has a specific age recommendation listed, ranging from picture-heavy toddler books to more text-rich early reader stories. We're adding new titles regularly!
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes! Through our Book Vault partnership we ship to over 150 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination and will be calculated at checkout.
